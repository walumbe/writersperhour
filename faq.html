Frequently Asked Questions

Company and Services

  • What services do you provide?

    Writers Per Hour is a reliable custom paper writing and editing service with a goal to help people with their academic writing needs. We provide the following services –

    • Writing papers from scratch
    • Editing
    • Proofreading
    • Rewriting
    • Formatting

  • What is your company responsible for?

    As soon as we receive the assignment details/brief from you, we find the best suited writer/editor from our large database of subject matter experts.

    Right through the process, we are available to offer you quality support 24/7. We proactively give you updates at every step of the ordering process related to plagiarism checks, proofreading and resolving disputes. Customer service is at the heart of everything we do, and we take full responsibility for on-time delivery and superior quality of the finished paper.

  • What if my writing assignment is very complex?

    Our team consists of writers who are experts in various kinds of writing ranging from academic writing, business writing and technical writing to creative writing and letter writing. Regardless of how specific or complex your assignment is, we have competent writers to help you with it.

    While filling the order form, you can choose the academic level (high school, undergrad, master’s, doctoral, IB) and click on the drop-down list to pick from our wide range of paper writing options. Be it the level, type of paper or citation – we have writers for every kind of requirement.

  • What do you mean by ‘IB level of writing’?

    IB level of writing only pertains to International Baccalaureate (IB) students who need help with their Theory of Knowledge essays, PPD forms, Internal Assessments, Extended Essays and other IB writing assignments.

    If you opt for the ‘IB level’, we can guarantee you that the assignment will be assigned to an IB qualified writer. However, if you opt for the ‘high school’ level, we will be unable to give you this assurance.

Guarantees

  • Do you guarantee confidentiality?

    Yes, we guarantee complete confidentiality. Our strict confidentiality policy ensures that your personal information is in secure hands and won’t be shared with any third parties.

    Not just that, the paper we write is exclusively meant for you and won’t be used by us or shared with anyone else. You can know more about our Privacy Policy here.

  • What if I am not satisfied with the final paper I received?

    Our company guarantees to deliver only top-notch quality academic and business papers by accurately matching your writing requirements to our subject matter experts. However, in case you are not satisfied with the final outcome, you may request for a FREE Revision (revision policy) and depending on the situation, we also offer partial or full refunds.

    To request for a revision:

    • Login to your account
    • Select the order number that needs revision
    • Click ‘Request Revision’ button on the right sidebar

    In order to request for a refund, send an e-mail to dispute@writersperhour.com. Request should be submitted in accordance with our money back policy otherwise it won't be accepted.

  • How can you guarantee my paper won’t be plagiarized?

    We understand the consequences of plagiarism and would never put our customers in an embarrassing situation which is why we have strict policies against plagiarism in place.

    We use several plagiarism checkers such as Turnitin and Plagscan among others to ensure we deliver 100% original papers. If required, we are also happy to provide a FREE plagiarism report.

  • How can you guarantee the quality of the paper?

    We guarantee to deliver papers of top-notch quality. In case you are not satisfied, you can ask for a free revision or full/partial refund.

    However, in order to ensure we submit high-quality work, we also need your support in sharing detailed instructions pertaining to your paper, while filling the order form. The more detailed and timelier your instructions are, the better the final output will be.

    We are in this together and rest assured, we do everything we can to ensure you are satisfied with the quality of the paper.

Payments & Pricing

  • How do you calculate the price for the assignment?

    We consider the following factors before deciding the price for the assignment –

    • Number of pages/words
    • Academic level of writing required – high school, IB, college, undergraduate, master, PhD, etc.
    • Urgency – shorter the deadline, higher the price

    Do note, that you will get approximately 250-275 words per page depending on the topic.

    Rest assured, we work to offer you the best price we can for the assignment.

    We understand people may have budget constraints and hence offer flexible payment terms. You do have the option of paying in installments.

    If you wish to avail this option, before placing the order, please contact our support team via email or live chat and discuss the process involved.

  • Can I get a custom price for my order?

    Yes, we do offer tailor-made, custom prices in the case of large orders such as dissertation paper writing and other vast projects.

    Yes, we offer the following services for free –

    • Plagiarism report

    • Step-by-step delivery which includes submitting drafts in parts to update you on the progress and quality of the paper
